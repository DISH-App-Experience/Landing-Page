FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DISH?

DISH is a product in which restaurant, bar, and convenience store owners can manage, edit, & publish their own apps. DISH clients are able to access many features for a flat fee, which is paid monthly. You have the access to control and update all realtime.

How long does it take to get set up?

Your restaurant app can be ready in anywhere from 3-5 business days. Everything is set up fairly quickly, although to be on the iOS App Store, there is a 1-2 business day wait for approval. You can download DISH Connect on the iOS App Store, where you'll manage your app.

How does DISH benefit me?

Not only will DISH provide an outlet for you to connect with customer and grow marketing opportunities, it also calls for customer retention. For restaurants in particular, mobile apps offer a ton of awesome feature to boost business in a cost-effective manner.

I'm a busy restaurant owner, is this time consuming?

Clients are able to edit their mobile app on the go, after signing in with their specific credentials. If they are interested in DISH to set up their menu automatically, clients can pay an additional $100 for a launch fee and automatic menu integration.
